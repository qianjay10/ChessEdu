Piece Development Exercise

Learn how to properly develop your pieces during the opening phase of the game

0/5 steps

Exercise Steps:

Step Information

Knights are typically the first pieces to develop in the opening. They can jump over other pieces and control important center squares.

Instructions:

Develop one of your knights to a good square. Knights should generally be developed towards the center of the board.

Move one of your knights to either f3 or c3
